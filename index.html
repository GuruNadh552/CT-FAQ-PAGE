    I am getting Invalid ID/ Password when logging in CT
  • Solution: Take the email ID of the student and check in our Manage User if it is registered.
  • If Yes, registered - He probably forgot the password or is entering the wrong password. Ask him to go to viteee.vit.ac.in and click on Forgot Password. He will receive a mail with the password.
  • If No, not registered (no results found) - Then his account is not registered in our platform yet. Ask him to connect with VITEEE team.
  • Background for *If No case* - The applicants create their acc on viteee and make the payment and are registered there firstly. VITEEE team then forwards us all the collected data and we register them in CT. This process of them transferring the data to us usually takes 24hrs and when we register their accs. Hence they will see Invalid login id/Password
  • What to tell them in *email ID not found* case - VIT has not yet sent the data to us yet. Once they send us, we will register and you can login and book your slot. For further info you can contact VITEEE
